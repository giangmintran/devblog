## Release pressure is real

Shipping deadlines can increase stress quickly. Without boundaries, teams accumulate fatigue and mistakes.

## Prevention checklist

- Define release owner and backup owner.
- Freeze non-critical scope 48 hours before launch.
- Keep rollback plan visible to everyone.
- Rotate on-call responsibilities.
- Run short check-ins for blockers.

## Personal guardrails

1. No late-night debugging two days in a row.
2. Keep meal and break schedules stable.
3. Write a short postmortem after release.

## What changed for my team

We reduced last-minute chaos and improved release confidence after adopting these rules.
