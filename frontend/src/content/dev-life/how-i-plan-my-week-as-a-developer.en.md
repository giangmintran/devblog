## Why weekly planning helps

Developers often lose time when priorities shift daily. A short planning ritual creates clarity and protects focus blocks.

## My 45-minute workflow

1. Review unfinished tasks from last week.
2. Pick one primary technical goal.
3. Define 2-3 secondary tasks.
4. Reserve deep work slots on calendar.
5. Leave one flexible block for urgent issues.

## Rules I follow

- Avoid overbooking Monday.
- Group meetings on specific days.
- Track energy, not only time.

## Outcome

I spend less time deciding what to do next and more time shipping meaningful work.
